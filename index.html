Receipts Space

Smart document collection for macOS. Organize receipts, invoices, and financial documents locally with complete privacy. OCR extracts key data, self-learning classification sorts everything effortlessly, and visual analytics reveal financial insights—all on your own device.

Local-First Privacy

All data stays on your Mac or personal storage like iCloud Drive or Dropbox. No cloud dependencies, no third-party access. Optional end-to-end encryption secures shared libraries across devices.

Intelligent Processing

Automatic OCR pulls amounts, dates, taxes, and bank details from PDFs, scans, emails, images, web pages, and electronic invoices. Self-learning system classifies documents to contacts and categories based on your habits. Duplicate detection keeps your library clean.

Effortless Workflow

Sync seamlessly via shared folders. Support for ScanSnap and hardware scanners. Direct invoice payments, automatic currency conversion, and GoBD-compliant audit mode with full change tracking. Export ready for accounting software.

Clear Financial Insights

Dashboard visualizes income, expenses, and trends. Open file format lets you access everything via Finder—independent of the app. Built for freelancers, small businesses, and privacy-focused users.

How It Works

1. Drag and Drop

Import receipts, invoices, scans, or emails directly into Receipts Space. Supports PDFs, images, web pages, and more.

2. OCR and Classify

Automatic text recognition extracts data. Self-learning AI assigns to categories, contacts, and tracks changes.

3. Sync and Analyze

Share via folders for multi-device access. View dashboard analytics, pay invoices, and export as needed.

4. Stay Compliant

GoBD audit mode logs everything. Encrypted libraries ensure security. Data always accessible in Finder.

FAQ

Is my data safe?

Yes. Local-first storage on your Mac or personal cloud. End-to-end encryption for shared access. No external servers.

What formats are supported?

PDFs, scanned documents, images, email attachments, web pages, XRechnung/ZUGFeRD invoices. Hardware scanners like ScanSnap included.

Does it sync across devices?

Perfectly via shared folders or iCloud Drive. Improved sync in version 3.0 handles everything smoothly.

What's new in 3.0?

Informative dashboard, GoBD compliance, encrypted libraries, better iCloud sync, and liquid glass design.

Do I need to buy anything?

No. This is the fully activated version of Receipts Space 3.0. Ready to use on macOS 14+.
